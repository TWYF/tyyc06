Q： How many TYYC divisions are open to contestants in 2006？
A： Sports Ladder, 1A, 2A, 4A and 5A divisions are open to contestants in 2006. 3A divisions is not included this year for it's still a low-developed art in Taiwan.

　　Sports Ladder is open to every player. The host of the contest believes that the Sports Ladder is not only competitive, but it also reveals the pleasure of yo-yoing
　　in yo-yo coaching.
 
Q： Does TYYC require the contestants to pay for admission's charge?
A： The admission's charge for 1A-5A is NTD250. Sports Ladder requires an admission's charge of NTD50. Any 1A-5A contestant are also sanctioned to compete
　 　 in Sports Ladder without being charged twice. However, contestants pay for Sport Ladder only are not permitted to compete in any division of 1A-5A.
　　 (A contestant, who had been charged for admission's charge of 1A, 2A, 4A, and 5A divisions, will not be charged twice for competeing in Sports Ladder.)

Q： What time will the event start?
A： The event starts at 9:00 o'clock in the morning Contestants may register for admissions at 9:00 o'clock in the morning. Each contestant will get a list of
　　compulsories and an identification after his admission's charge was cleared. After contestant got his trick list, he may start completing his list in front of a
　　monitor.The general freestyles will begin at 12:00 o'clock in the noon. The compulsories are 10% of contestant's final scores. Contestants are required to
　　complete the compulsories before the general freestyles begin. Contestants will automatically lose the rest of the points for compulsories on the list after 12:00
　　o'clock in the noon. There will be no more judging of compulsories tricks after 12:00 o'clock. The event will end at 19:00 o'clock in night. TYYC suggests all
　　contestant who lives away from Taipei City to be carefull about their own time.
 

Q： How should I prepare for my freestyle music?
A： Please turn in your CDs to the staff after you have all your balance(The Admission's Charge) cleared. TYYC only accept regular CDs or CDs in MP3s and
　　WMA. Please note that TYYC would not accept any musics that had been stored in any type of portable device. Contestants are required to transfer a track into
　　the recognized format of TYYC, or to select a track in the format that is recognized by TYYC. Contestant are suggested to contact TYYC immediately if any
　　assistance on file transferring is necessary.
 

Q： How should I deal with emergencies?
A： There will be a monitor that is responsible for any usual situations on that day. The monitor will be very noticeable because he will put an identification on.
　　Please contact the monitor as soon as you can if you have found out any unusual situations. The monitor will offer immediate and appropriate assistance. Please
　　note that the monitor will no longer be available if there were disasters like a fire, an earthquake or a terrorist's attack.
 

Q： What time will the contestants know their final results?
A：The score of the compulsories will be shown to contestant immediately after the contestant completed his lists of compulsories. The list of compulsories will be
　　given to contestant after his admission's charge was cleared. Each contestant's score of compulsories will be pasted on a bulletin board at 13:00 p.m. afternoon.
　　The final results of general freestyles and Soprts Ladder will be shown to contestants after general freestyles section was ended. All prizes will be given to
　　awarded contestants by TYYC at that time.
 

Q： Are contestants allowed to use a yo-yo that had been modified?

A： A modified yo-yo is generally welcomed. As long as the yo-yo still remain the basic functions of a yo-yo, TYYC welcome the contestant to compete using this
　　yo-yo. If you aren't sure whether your yo-yo is accepted, a judge will be available to test your yo-yo and make sure it is accepted. The contestants in Artistic
　　Performances are allowed to use any property to support his freestyle.
