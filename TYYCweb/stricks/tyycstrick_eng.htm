Compulsories will be 10% of final grades. There are 3 chances of failing for each trick. If contestant
performed a perfect move(complete the trick in the first attempt), he will get 3 points. A second attempt of
each trick is for 2 points, and a third attempt is for 1 point. If contestant failed in the third attempt, he will
be considered a fail of attempting that trick. Therefore, he will get 0 point for that trick. For sports ladder,
contestants are required to complete the hyper tricks list in the order written on the list. Each contestant has
2 chances to complete a trick. If a contestant failed in the second attempt, he will be asked to move to the
next trick on the list until he completed the trick list. The judge will count the grade the contestant got .
according to how many times the contestant attempt on one trick
