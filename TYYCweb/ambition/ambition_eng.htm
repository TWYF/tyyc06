Competitive Yo-yo has been developed for almost 40 years. The first generation is called the period of "Zhir Lan Yo-yo". The style of yo-yoing was not as varied as it

is today. Taiwanese are always following after whatever that is a fashion outside of Taiwan. The first group of people gave "Yo-yo" Chinese name, which is "Ryu

Ryu Chyu", without much thinking involved in. It is a childish name. Yo-yo has been an entertainment for all age in Taiwan for generations. For people who was

born in 1960, yo-yos live in their mind. However, yo-yo is never something big in Taiwan. The touch of yo-yo just faded away.

However, a new concept of designing yo-yo re-generate the yo-yo community in Taiwan. A yo-yo with a ball bearing, revolutionized the yo-yo industry in the 90's.
During the end of the 90's, many yo-yo factories sponsored events in order to promote yo-yos. The promotion affected Taiwanese players, of course. Unfortunately,
the promotion during the end of 90's didn't last for long. After few years, time came to the year 2001. Few generous players start their endeavor of yo-yo promotion.
They will be holding the first official national contest, which is the "TYYC" in 2006, after few times of experimental events were held. The career goal of TYYC is to
provide an international, professional environment for players to participate and to learn from each other. TYYC also encourages players to strive for Taiwanese yo-yo
community. The most important tenet of TYYC is to present a new concept and a revolutionized style of yo-yoing in public.
