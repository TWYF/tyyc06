The first Taiwanese Yo-Yo Contest(TYYC) will be held at August, 20, 2006
TYYC has 5 categories. They are 1A(Sigle hand string tricks divison), 2A(double hand looping tricks division), 4A(Offstring tricks division), 5A(Freehand tricks
division) and Basic Division,Basic Division is open to beginners.
The contest will begin at 10 o'clock. You can refer to the official website for further informations about rules if you would need.
 

The 2006 TYYC comtestant scored the highest in each division will be given a

delicate hand-made medal. Other awards for each division are also readied. All
contestants with their admission's charge clear will be given a delicate present
only available at the venue.
The emblem that is going to be distributed at the venue by the day the event started.
